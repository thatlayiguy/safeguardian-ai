Email and SMS scams, otherwise called phishing and smishing respectively, are a form of cybercrime that involves sending fraudulent messages to trick recipients into revealing sensitive information or installing malware. They are one of the most common and costly cyber threats, affecting individuals, businesses and organizations because they are easy, cheap, and effective for cybercriminals to use. However, there is usually more awareness about phishing as opposed to smishing. This is the reason you are probably hearing "smishing" for the first time :)

Project

The purpose of my project is to raise awareness to and also help contribute to combating cybercrime specifically phishing and smishing. SafeGuardian AI is a Machine Learning Model trained on a dataset of about six thousand messages to identify and detect scam content in emails and text messages. Currently, the model can detect scam content accurately in text messages. However, while it can detect scam content in emails to some level of accuracy, the dataset on which the model is trained does not natively contain enough of emails. Therefore, the model still needs to undergo further training with an email based dataset. For now, the model can be accessed from a Web App. Feel free to try it out!
